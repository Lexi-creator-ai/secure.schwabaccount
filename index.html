Frequently Asked Questions

What are the hours for phone support?

Our brokerage and general support line is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. Specialized services like banking may have extended hours—call 1-800-435-4000 to connect instantly.

How do I transfer funds between my Schwab accounts?

Easily move money via our online platform or app. For phone assistance, our reps can guide you through ACH transfers, wire instructions, or internal account linkages securely and efficiently.

Can I get help with retirement planning?

Absolutely! Discuss IRAs, 401(k) rollovers, and personalized retirement strategies with our certified advisors. We're committed to helping you build a future-proof plan.
